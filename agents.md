# AGENTS.md

## Adding Blog Content

1. Create a new markdown file in `src/content/posts/`
2. Add metadata at the top:
   ```markdown
   # Your Article Title
   
   **Classification:** CONFIDENTIEL  
   **Date de déclassification:** DD/MM/YYYY  
   **Source:** Your Source
   
   ## Your Content
   ```

You may choose random dates within the past 3 years for the **Date de déclassification:** DD/MM/YYYY  unless otherwise specified by the user

Source and Classification are also fields that should be edited for every post
